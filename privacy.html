Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 4, 2025

At TAO Studios, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you.

1. Data We Collect

We primarily collect anonymous, non-personal data necessary for game operation and improvement, such as:

  • **Roblox User IDs:** Used for in-game moderation and leaderboards.
  • **In-Game Activity:** Actions, timestamps, and game statistics.
  • **Device Information:** Device type and operating system version for bug fixing.

2. How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Improving our games and services.
  • Analyzing player engagement and statistics.
  • Ensuring compliance with our game rules (moderation).

3. Third-Party Services

Our games operate within the Roblox platform, which has its own privacy policy. We do not share the limited data we collect with any other third-party services outside of the Roblox ecosystem.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our official Discord server linked on our website.
